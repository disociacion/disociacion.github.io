about

Born in Buenos Aires, the artist Disociación is a producer and DJ influenced by genres such as EBM, electro, break and noise. Her live set is intended to take the listener on a journey through the corners of their mind that can only be accessed through distorted and hypnotic sounds that give rise to a magnetising sense of dysphoria.

She has released his EP Mental Massive Control in the year 2020 via the argentinian label Of Dolls and Murder, and has participated in several VA's of world renowned labels such as Pildoras Tapes, Idlestates Recordings, Intheirfeelings, Deskonfiguracion, etc, and in many Podcast series of different labels as a dj. She had the opportunity to play in cities like BA, Santiago, Barcelona and Zürich alongside established artists such as Years of Denial, RNXRX, Pulsum, Andrea Paz and Daniele Cosmos.
